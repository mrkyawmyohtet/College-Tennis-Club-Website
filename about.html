About our Tennis Club...

Our tennis club was first formed by our senior students from various majors since 2013. A group of friends who love to play tennis in their free times got the first idea of this club. They used to play tennis in local tennis areas but they thought that would be nice to have a community of students who love to play tennis like them in our college. And then they spoke with the teachers and principle of the colleg and they agreeded on the idea and this community and this club came alive since then.
Our community first prioritzes to create a happy and healthy environment for every student who love to play tennis in our college. We also try to participate in tournaments to communicate with other schools' clubs and enlarge our community.

  • We create club activities once a week or depending to our academic schedules (eg. exams, lectures etc) to create a strong community and have a proper communication within the club members.
  • Those who want to become expert in tennis sport can train with coaches additionally in weekend. There will be a little fund for our coaches, just 5 bucks for a month as they have to come to school in their leisure times and they receive no charge from school for this extra training time.
  • To make sure the community and the club is the best for students, we always welcome any kind of feedback or comment about our club or our community. So if you have any suggestion or something to complain, please feel free to talk to us or email us. We will not reveal your identity and will consider your thoughts and ideas.
